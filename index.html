The Official Daemonic Sword Website

Welcome to the official Daemonic Sword ORPG Website. Here you can download the latest version of the map and learn about the game, characters, and secrets strategies. You can also join in on our forum or Discord server to post comments and chat about the game.

The Daemonic Sword is an ORPG like no other. Expore the land of Brenellia by yourself or with a group of friends. Fight through the Good or Evil questline. Venture beyond the end of the world to fight lots of challenging bosses. Find out the truth about yourself and save the world from impending doom. Play as 14 unique characters, each with their own evolved form. There is a dedicated community working on improving the game, so be sure to check for updates every now and then.
